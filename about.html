About Me

A Hamilton-based graphic designer focused on bringing old black & white photos back to life, image editing, and television graphics. The goal of my work is to create unique and effective solutions to any design challenge thrown my way. This entails, finding the best way to unite clients' desire with the expertise of my design experience.

My professional work experience includes working at Business News Network, CTV local news, TSN sports, and an assortment of freelance projects.
